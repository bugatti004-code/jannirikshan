Process
How JanNirikshan Works
A transparent 4-step pipeline that turns citizen observations into government action.
01

Capture & Upload

Citizens photograph or video government services — roads, drainage, lights, or public facilities — and upload with location.

02

AI Analysis

Computer vision detects defects, scores quality, and classifies issue severity — objectively, without bias.

03

Report Compiled

A structured report with evidence, findings, geo-location, and citizen input is automatically generated.

04

Forwarded to Dept

Verified reports are dispatched to the concerned ministry with a tracking ID for accountability.
